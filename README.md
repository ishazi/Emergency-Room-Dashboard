# 🏥 Emergency Room Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📊 Project Overview

This project presents an **Emergency Room (ER) Dashboard** built using Power BI to perform **descriptive analysis** on hospital data collected over a **19-month period (April 2023 – October 2024)**.

The dataset contains **9,216 unique patient records** and provides insights into patient flow, satisfaction levels, departmental referrals, and demographic patterns. The goal of this dashboard is to support **data-driven decision-making** to improve hospital efficiency and patient care.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze patient wait times and satisfaction levels
* Identify peak busy periods in the emergency room
* Understand referral patterns across departments
* Examine patient demographics (age and race)
* Evaluate admission vs discharge trends

---

## 📌 Key Metrics & Insights

### ⏱️ Patient Wait Time & Satisfaction

* **Average Wait Time:** 35.3 minutes

  * Indicates a need for improved patient flow and efficiency
* **Average Satisfaction Score:** 4.99 / 10

  * Reflects moderate satisfaction and highlights areas for improvement in patient experience

---

### 🏥 Department Referrals

* **No Referral Required:** 5,400 patients
* **Most Common Referrals:**

  * General Practice: 1,840 cases
  * Orthopedics: 995 cases
  * Physiotherapy: 276 cases
  * Cardiology: 248 cases

👉 Insight: General Practice and Orthopedics handle the majority of referred cases.

---

### 📅 Peak Busy Periods

#### 📌 Busiest Days:

* Monday: 1,377 patients
* Saturday: 1,322 patients
* Tuesday: 1,318 patients

#### ⏰ Busiest Hours:

* 11:00 AM
* 1:00 PM
* 7:00 PM
* 11:00 PM

👉 Insight: These peak periods require **adequate staffing and resource allocation**.

---

### 👥 Patient Demographics

#### 📊 Age Distribution:

* 30–39 years: 1,200 patients (largest group)
* 20–29 years: 1,188 patients
* 40–50 years: Significant representation

#### 🌍 Race Distribution:

* White: 2,571 patients
* African American: 1,951 patients
* Multiracial: 1,557 patients
* Asian: 1,060 patients
* Declined to specify: 1,030 patients

👉 Insight: The ER serves a **diverse population** across age and racial groups.

---

### 🏥 Admission Patterns

* **Admitted Patients:** 4,612
* **Treated & Released:** 4,604

👉 Insight: Nearly **50% of patients require admission**, indicating high case severity or need for further care.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

* KPI Cards for key metrics (patients, wait time, satisfaction)
* Charts for patient trends and demographics
* Time-based analysis for peak hours and days
* Department referral breakdown
* Admission vs discharge comparison

---

## 💡 Summary

The analysis reveals:

* High patient volumes across the observed period
* Moderate patient satisfaction levels
* Frequent referrals to General Practice and Orthopedics
* Peak activity on Mondays and during late hours
* A diverse patient demographic profile
* Nearly equal proportions of admitted and discharged patients

---

## 🚀 Conclusion

This dashboard provides valuable insights into emergency room operations and highlights opportunities to:

* Improve patient wait times
* Enhance service quality and satisfaction
* Optimize staffing during peak periods
* Allocate resources effectively across departments
